Dark mode is no longer just a trend; it's a fundamental part of the modern web experience. Designing a great dark mode requires more than just swapping colors.

## Best Practices

- **Avoid Pure Black**: Use dark greys for depth.
- **Limit Saturated Colors**: Use muted tones to avoid eye strain.
- **Ensure Contrast**: Always check WCAG guidelines.
